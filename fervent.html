Been months since I'd seen a newspaper that wasn't covered in rotting food or shit. Years if I included the ones that had been little more than fire fodder.

The top right corner said Thursday 24th of December 2015.

It smelled musky and old. LA Times. It was a long way from home.

"Wonder why they kept it"

Dorian shrugged. Not his problem.

I rolled up the paper and stuffed it into my back pocket.
